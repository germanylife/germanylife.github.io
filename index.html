Introduction

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") describes how we process personal data of users of the mobile application "Germany Life" (the "App"). This document has been prepared in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) of the European Union and the German data protection laws.

The "Germany Life" app is an educational and informational resource for immigrants and residents of Germany, providing information on the "Leben in Deutschland" and "Einbürgerungstest" exams, tips for adaptation, waste sorting, useful phrases, and directories of banks and services, among others. The app is developed by an independent developer and is not affiliated with any government bodies in Germany.

By using the App, the user agrees to the terms of this Policy. If the user does not agree with the terms, they should discontinue using the App.

Collection and Use of Personal Data

Data Collection when Using the App. At the moment, using "Germany Life" does not require registration or the provision of any personal data. The app does not request personal information such as your name, address, or date of birth, nor does it collect such data automatically when used normally.

No Sensitive Data Collection. The app also does not request access to sensitive data on the user's device (e.g., contacts, photos, or geolocation) and does not receive such data without explicit permission. All features of the app are provided without the need to enter personal information.

User Inquiries. If a user voluntarily contacts the developer of the App (e.g., by email for support or feedback), such communication may contain personal data (e.g., email address or name). This information will only be used to respond to the inquiry and will not be shared with third parties, except as required by law.

Future Changes in Data Collection. If new features or services are added to the App that involve the input or processing of personal data (e.g., creating an account, personalized settings, etc.), we will notify users in advance and update this Privacy Policy accordingly. Any future processing of personal data will be carried out based on legal grounds (such as user consent or legitimate interests) and in strict compliance with GDPR.

Third-Party Services and Analytics

We will seek users' necessary consent for the collection of analytics data if required by applicable laws, or we will rely on our legitimate interest while respecting the rights and freedoms of the user.

Right to Withdraw Consent. If the processing of personal data is based on the user's consent, they have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawing consent will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.
